Who we are

Here at Jurassic Outfitting Co., we aim to provide clothing and equipment of the highest possible quality so that visitors are well-equipped to explore what Jurassic Park has to offer. Our company provides all of the clothing and equipment necessary for the various expeditions offered in the park. To accomplish this, our staff works in close partnership with Jurassic Adventure Tours to formulate the best products and packages for their expeditions. We hope to continue creating and preserving the feelings of security and satisfaction park visitors feel knowing that they are well prepared for their adventures.

Our Story

In 1980, our company's founders Taha Shahid and Mike Howell started creating custom clothes and gear for close friends, family, and coworkers to finance as well as support their outdoor activities. Word spread about the well-crafted custom products to professional outdoors-men, leading our founders to realize that the market lacked products catered to professionals, instead focusing on providing stylish products to the general public that lacked functionality. After this realization, Taha and Michael became well-known throughout the world for their incredibly functional gear. When the construction of Jurassic Park began in 1987, the owner of the park, John Hammond, reached out to our founders about an opportunity to create an outfitting business for the park. Working closely with Hammond and Jurassic Adventure Tours, our founders dedicated themselves to creating a business that would ensure park visitors were fully equipped during their expeditions.
